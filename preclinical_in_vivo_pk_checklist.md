**Dose key reporting details checklist:**

	**🙾** Route of Administration

	**🙾** Information regarding salt form of compound batch used, if applicable

**🙾** Information regarding solid form of compound batch used, if applicable, along with any supporting data (PLM, PXRD, etc.) used to determine amorphous/crystalline nature,

	**🙾** Dose Level with both Dose Concentration and Dose Volume

	**🙾** Formulation vehicle details with clear procedure for preparation

  **🙾** Formulation condition after compound dissolution (solution, suspension, etc.)

	**🙾** Fed/Fasted state of animal

**Clearance key reporting details checklist:**

	**🙾** Dose information for IV experiment used to calculate Clearance

	**🙾** Matrix used for collection (often plasma or blood)

	**🙾** Fraction unbound in plasma to aid with understanding CLu

	**🙾** BPR, especially if using blood as the matrix and desiring fu,b, or if calculating FaFg from CLp

	**🙾** AUClast and AUCinf 

	**🙾** Assumed value of Qh if reporting extraction ratios or an FaFg

**Volume of Distribution key reporting details checklist:**

	**🙾** All relevant details from the IV experiment used to calculate Volume of Distribution,  
    including key Clearance details described above.

	**🙾** Clarity regarding which specific Volume of Distribution is being reported (usually Vss)  
	  
	**🙾** Version of software used to calculate C0 and Volumes of Distribution using curve-fitting
